A quick bit

About Me

Hello there! Over the past 5-6 years, I have worn many hats, both literally and figuratively. I have worked with various projects and companies, primarily in the transportation, construction, fashion, and food industries. Some testimonials are above, and more can be found on LinkedIn.

I began my career as a graphic designer, creating logos, print designs, and various brand materials. My position quickly transitioned to working as a dedicated User Experience & User Interface (UX/UI) designer, where I designed and project-managed features for a web application at a successful Pittsburgh SaaS startup called Idelic. The web app (Safety Suite) utilizes machine learning to analyze the safety of CDL truck drivers. During my time there, I was not just doing UX and Product design but also engaged in business analysis and product management by writing product requirements, user stories, and understanding and mapping out customers' business operations when needed. I worn many hats during my time there.

I've also had the pleasure of assisting tech startup founders in learning basic Python programming through an in-person artificial intelligence course in the evenings that my Javascript teacher encouraged me to help with.

Later, I transitioned to my current role, where I have decided to specialize in designing and building E-commerce and lead generation sites using Shopify and Webflow, respectively.

In addition to my love for web design and development, I've found a passion for sharing my knowledge and experiences through podcasting. Currently, I host the Deno Developer Show and Tensorflow Developer Show. The Deno Developer show focuses on a newer Javascript runtime that competes with Node.js. It's included popular micro-influences in the Javascript community like Nick Nisi from one of the most popular Javascript podcasts today, JS Party; Kitson Kelly, formerly a Deno core team member and Dojo project lead; and Luca Casonato, a TC39 delegate and Deno core team member. The Tensorflow Developer show is a much newer podcast that focuses on introducing AI, ML, and Tensorflow concepts to developers new to machine learning.
